## Laura Ramírez

### Hi! Welcome to my portfolio

I'm a Bogotá based art director and branding specialist with a systematic and strategic perspective on design, my strengths are the creation of coherent and versatile visual systems driven by precise composition, outstanding typography use and strong graphic identities.

I have over 5 years of professional experience in the fields of communication design, brand identity and editorial design. My clients have spanned from major national firms to individuals in healthcare, banking, politics, hospitality, technology, activism and non-profits, among others.
